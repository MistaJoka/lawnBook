# Project Intake

## 1. App Snapshot

### Beginner Questions
- What is the app called? LawnBook
- What is the app in one sentence? AI mobile app for hard working landscapers.
- Who is the main user? solo landscapers with small operations and businesses. 
- What problem does it solve? saves time and confusion. gaining premium features as lower costs
- What does the user do before this app exists? manual, switches between providers. 
- What does the user do after this app exists? Save time, earn more, ease workflows. 

### Advanced Questions
- What market gap does this app target? Small business owners that don't want to spend what the top providers are asking for their platforms. 
- What existing tools does this replace or simplify? Jobber, Yardbook, RealGreen
- What is the user's current workaround? Spending too much time makeing calls to organize jobs and collect customer data and payment. 
- Why would someone pay for this instead of using spreadsheets, notes, or existing software? Cheaper since it is being vibe coded. Powered by gemini api key. 
- What would make this app feel obviously useful in the first 5 minutes? Able to see what is currently active, pending needs review, future. money collected recently. 

## 2. Target User

### Beginner Questions
- Who uses this daily? landscaper on the job and in the fields. 
- How technical is this person? They know what AI is but is unable to use LLMs confidently.
- What device do they use most? Smartphone S26 Ultra
- Where are they physically when using it? On the road, in a yard or field.
- What frustrates them most right now? Not being organized and having to put a lot of time and effort into keeping the jobs and money optimized. 

### Advanced Questions
- What emotional state is the user usually in while using the app? In-tuned with their goal and next objective.
- What distractions exist during real-world use? Seeing more information than what is needed for that part.
- What user mistakes should the app prevent? Entering non-valid field entries. Numbers where letters should be. continuing without critical data missing.
- What information does the user need quickly? Name, contact, location, 2 word job description, and quote.
- What should be usable with one hand? scrolling, text entry, field selection and basic entries.
