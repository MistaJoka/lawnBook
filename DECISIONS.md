# Architecture and Product Decisions

Use this file to record important choices and prevent AI drift.

## Decision Template

```md
## YYYY-MM-DD — Decision Title

### Decision
What was decided?

### Reason
Why was this chosen?

### Alternatives Considered
What else was considered?

### Consequences
What does this affect later?
```
